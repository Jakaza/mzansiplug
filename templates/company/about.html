About Mzansi Plug

Who We Are

Mzansi Plug is a South African platform built to help people find real jobs and ways to earn money. We know it can be hard to find trusted work online, so we made a website that brings everything together in one place — for free.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, we started because we saw too many South Africans struggling to find legitimate work opportunities online. Our team includes job seekers, career coaches, and tech experts who understand the challenges of unemployment in South Africa. We've helped over 15,000 job seekers connect with employers and have verified more than 3,000 job listings across the country.

What makes us different is that we're built by South Africans, for South Africans. We understand the local job market, the challenges of transport, the importance of community, and the need for opportunities that fit our unique circumstances.

How We Help South Africans

  • Verified Job Listings: Real jobs from trusted sources, verified by our team before they go live.
  • Local Opportunities: Jobs shared by location with info on transport access.
  • Side Hustles: Part-time gigs, surveys, weekend work, and seasonal jobs.
  • Career Advice: CV writing, interview prep, salary tips, and more.
  • No Experience Needed: Listings for youth and first-time job seekers.
  • Skills Development: Info on free and affordable training courses.
  • Community Support: Forums, Q&A, and success stories to stay inspired.

Industries We Cover

We feature opportunities in retail, hospitality, healthcare, education, agriculture, security, construction, call centers, and more. We also cover digital, freelance, and seasonal work across South Africa.

Our Mission

To make it easier for South Africans to find work, grow income, and build better futures. We want to remove the barriers that prevent people from accessing real opportunities.

Why We Do It

Because too many people waste time or money on fake job offers. Mzansi Plug was created to be the change — a trusted source of verified, useful opportunities.

We’ve seen how lives change when someone gets their first job or earns enough to support their family. We believe in the power of opportunity to uplift communities.

How We Verify Jobs

We verify every listing manually: checking company info, talking to employers, and screening for red flags like fake salaries or requests for banking details.

Our Promise

Mzansi Plug will always be free for job seekers. We will verify every job, listen to your feedback, and continue improving. You are not just a user — you’re a member of our community.

Looking Forward

We’re building more tools to help: WhatsApp alerts, mobile apps, CV builders, interview schedulers, and more training partnerships.

Get Involved

Employers, mentors, and job referrers — you’re welcome too. Share opportunities, guide others, or partner with us to uplift our people.
