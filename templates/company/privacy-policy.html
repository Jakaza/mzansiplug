Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective Date: June 2025

At Mzansi Plug, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information about you when you use our website, mobile application, and related services.

By using Mzansi Plug, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services to South African job seekers and entrepreneurs.

Personal Information You Provide
  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password, and profile details
  • Profile Data: Skills, work experience, education, location, and career preferences
  • Resume/CV Information: Employment history, qualifications, and professional achievements
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, and support requests
  • Application Information: Job applications, cover letters, and application responses
Information Collected Automatically
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, search queries, and interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address (with your consent)
  • Cookies and Tracking: Website preferences, session data, and analytics information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for legitimate business purposes to enhance your job search experience and platform functionality.

Purpose Information Used Legal Basis
Account Management Personal details, login credentials Contract performance
Job Matching Skills, experience, preferences Legitimate interest
Communication Contact information, preferences Consent
Platform Improvement Usage data, feedback Legitimate interest
Security & Fraud Prevention Device info, behavior patterns Legal obligation

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information in specific circumstances to provide our services effectively.

When We Share Information
  • With Employers: Your profile and application information when you apply for jobs
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate our platform
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or regulatory authorities
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information
Your Control

You have complete control over your profile visibility. You can choose to make your profile searchable by employers, keep it private, or share it selectively. We never share your information without your knowledge and consent.

Data Security and Protection

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Security Measures
  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access controls and authentication systems
  • Regular Audits: Routine security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Data Backup: Secure backup systems and disaster recovery procedures
  • Third-Party Security: Verified security standards for all service providers
Important Notice

While we implement robust security measures, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords, keep your login credentials confidential, and report any suspicious activity immediately.

Your Privacy Rights

Under South African privacy laws, including the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), you have specific rights regarding your personal information.

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you

Right to Rectification

Correct or update inaccurate or incomplete information

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal information under certain conditions

Right to Object

Object to the processing of your personal information

Data Portability

Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format

Right to Restrict

Limit how we process your personal information

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and improve our services.

Types of Cookies We Use
  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how you use our website
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Provide insights into website usage and performance

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling certain cookies may limit website functionality.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

Retention Periods
  • Active Accounts: Information retained while your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data may be retained for up to 3 years after last activity
  • Application Data: Job applications retained for 2 years unless requested for deletion
  • Communication Records: Support communications retained for 1 year
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

Children's Privacy

Mzansi Plug is designed for adults seeking employment opportunities. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 years of age.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 18, we will take immediate steps to delete such information from our records.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors.

  • Significant changes will be communicated via email or prominent website notices
  • The effective date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last updated
  • Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
  • We encourage you to review this policy regularly
